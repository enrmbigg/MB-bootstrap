Club Sessions

We are all volunteers! and are involved in the Oval Ski Club because of our interest in the sport.

Club Sessions

The club holds sessions for both Juniors and Adults and covers the full spectrum of ability, from first timers to seasoned racers. Everyone is welcome to join the club to ski. If you are a beginner then please check the diary for the start date of the next set of beginners lessons. As we are a members only club everyone must join prior to skiing (see costs below). This only takes a few minutes and can be done at your first visit. Please remember that we are a voluntary, none profit making organisation, so all money raised through membership is used for equipment, instructor training etc so everyone benefits. You will also receive discounts from various local sports shops.
