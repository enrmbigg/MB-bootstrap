Walk to Hilbre Island - Sat 14th July, 1.30pm, Starting at Dee Lane Slipway, West Kirby (nr Morrison's car park)
The walk to Hilbre Island is 2 miles each way and we will be crossing over at low tide however sensible clothing and footwear is recommended. All are welcome including non members.
Club AGM, Oval Sports Centre - Sunday 15th July, at 5.00pm
Would you like your say on how your ski club is run; have you any suggestions or questions relating to the skiing sessions, training, teaching, holidays, racing or social events? Well this is your big chance. The Oval Ski Club's AGM is taking place on the 15th July at the Oval Sports Centre Meeting room (within the sports centre) at 5pm. If that was not incentive enough the club will be providing free refreshments!!! (drinks and nibbles) Remember the club is run entirely by volunteers without whom none of the above activities would take place, further volunteers are very much in demand, so if you can spare a few hours making drinks on a Saturday morning or would like to be Chairman (or any of the jobs in between) - any new volunteers would be extremely welcome. All committee posts will be up for election. If you interested in attending or would like more information please contact Jane by either phone or email .
